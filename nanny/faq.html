FAQ

What happened if I have already paid for the services but decided to cancel it?

In order to be fair to the service providers, cancellation made at least 12 hours before the time of service is allowed. You will be entitled to a Full refund, less off the service and transaction fees which amounts to 10% of the payment.

For cancellation made in less than 12 hours to the time of service, cancellation is allowed only if the service provider is agreeable to it. In event the service provider is agreeable to a cancellation made in less than 12 hours, you will be entitled a Full refund, less off the service and transaction fees which amounts to 10% of the payment. Otherwise, the service provider will received the full payment.

What happened if I have already paid but wanted to change the date of the service?

In order to be fair to the service provider, you are allowed to change the date once, subject to the availability of the service provider. The revised date cannot be more than 2 months from the original date.

What happened if halfway through the provision of service such as nanny services, I wanted to terminate his/her service?

We do not interfere with such dispute. However, in event both parties have mutually agreed on a lower fees as a result of the termination, the platform will provide the refund accordingly, less off the transaction fees of the original payment.

What happened if my service provider did not turned up?

Full refund will be made to you. You will not be charged with the service and transaction fees which amounts to 10% of the payment.

What happened if my service provider cancelled the appointment?

We would strongly advice the service provider not to cancel the appointment at a very last minute. However, in event of unforeseen circumstances whereby the appointment is cancelled at a last minute, full refund will be made to you. You will not be charged with the service and transaction fees which amounts to 10% of the payment.
